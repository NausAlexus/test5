What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and providing you with personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us measure site performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enhance personalization and functionality.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used for targeted advertising.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers provide options to block or delete cookies.
