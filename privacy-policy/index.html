Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines our procedures regarding the collection, usage, and disclosure of your information in connection with our fantasy football service. We aim to inform you of your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Definitions and Interpretation

Interpretation

Words with their first letter capitalized carry meanings defined in the following sections. The given definitions will hold the same meaning regardless of their plurality.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • User Account refers to a unique account established to access our services or parts of our offerings.

  • Affiliated Entity indicates an organization that has controlling influence over, is influenced by, or is under common control with a party.

  • Company refers to BatBrigade, located in Mumbai, India.

  • Cookies are small files placed on your device that contain browsing details for the website.

  • Location refers to Maharashtra, India.

  • Device indicates any electronic device that can access the service.

  • Personal Information includes any details that can identify an individual.

  • Service pertains to the BatBrigade website.

  • Data Processor refers to any individual or organization that manages data on behalf of the company.

  • Activity Data refers to data collected automatically during service use.

  • You implies the person accessing or using the service.

Collection and Use of Your Personal Information

Types of Data Collected

Personal Information

While using our services, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable details that can be used for contact or identification.

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Contact number
  • Activity Data

Activity Data

Activity Data is gathered automatically when utilizing the service. This data may include information such as your device's IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on those pages.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to monitor activities on our service and store certain information.

  • Cookies: A small file stored on your device. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies.
  • Web Beacons: Small electronic files that allow us to count visits and understand statistics.

Using Your Personal Information

The Company may utilize personal information for the following purposes:

  • To operate our service: Including monitoring usage.
  • To manage your account: Enabling access to features available to registered users.
  • Contract performance: Implementing agreements related to our products or services.
  • To communicate with you: Via email, calls, text, or notifications regarding our services.
  • For business purposes: Evaluating or executing potential business changes.
  • For analytics: Evaluating usage trends and improving our offerings.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Data Processors: To monitor or analyze service usage.
  • During business transfers: In case of mergers or acquisitions.
  • With Affiliates: Requiring compliance with this Privacy Policy.
  • With partners: To offer certain services or promotions.
  • With other users: When you share information publicly.
  • With your consent: For any other purposes as agreed.

Retention of Your Personal Information

The Company will retain your personal data only as long as necessary. Data may be retained to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

Transfer of Your Personal Information

Your data may be processed in locations outside your residential jurisdiction. Your consent to this Privacy Policy indicates your agreement to such transfers.

Deleting Your Personal Information

You have the right to request the deletion of personal data we have collected about you. You may also request to access or correct your information.

Disclosure of Your Personal Information

Business Transactions

If we are involved in a merger or acquisition, your personal data may be transferred to another entity.

Law Enforcement

The Company may be obliged to disclose your personal information in response to lawful requests.

Other Legal Requirements

Additionally, the Company may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, protect rights, or ensure safety.

Security of Your Personal Information

Your data security is paramount; however, please acknowledge that no digital storage method is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not intentionally collect personal data from minors.

Third-party Links

Our services may have links to third-party websites. We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these sites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email.
