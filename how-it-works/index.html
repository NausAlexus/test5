1. Download & Register

To get started with Sportiverse, download the app from the Apple Store or Google Play. The installation process is quick and easy.

Once the app is installed, create your account by entering your email or phone number. You'll also need to set a secure password. After registration, you'll have access to all the features of the app.

2. Build Your Dream Team

Creating your fantasy team is easy and fun! Follow these steps to assemble a winning squad and dominate the competition.

Select a Match

Choose from upcoming matches in cricket, football, kabaddi, and more. Pick a game that suits your strategy.

Pick Your Players

Select players within your budget. Analyze their recent performances, stats, and roles to make the best choices.

Assign Captain & Vice-Captain

Choose a captain (2x points) and vice-captain (1.5x points) to maximize your team's potential.

Save Your Team

Finalize your team before the match starts. You can make changes until the deadline.
